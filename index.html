About

A winding path into software — and I wouldn't trade any of the detours.

I didn't start out headed for code. I got my start in sports — playing, coaching, and managing facilities — and earned a Bachelor's in Sports Management with a Business minor. When my sons came along, I moved into construction, working as a Project Inspector for INDOT. But technology had always pulled at my curiosity in the background, and eventually I stopped putting it off: I earned a certification in software engineering through TripleTen and started building.

Outside of code, I'm still on the sidelines coaching, tinkering with 3D printing, and keeping busy as a husband and father of four sons. I'm also a certified Firefighter I & II with First Aid/CPR certification — training that, like this new career, mostly comes down to staying calm and working the problem in front of you.
